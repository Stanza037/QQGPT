Diploma Course Examinations

State Board of Technical Education & Training

Attendance Policy

The academic year runs from June to March. Classes are held Monday to Saturday, with 8 periods daily.

  • 75% attendance is mandatory for end exams
  • 65%-75% with medical condonation
  • Below 65% results in detention

Examination Scheme

Comprehensive evaluation through theory and practical assessments:

  • Theory: 40% external + 60% internal
  • Practical: 40% external + 60% internal

Credit System

Accumulate credits through various course components:

  • Theory: 3 credits per subject
  • Laboratory: 1.5 credits per session
  • Skill Development: 2.5 credits per module

Total 130 credits required for diploma completion
